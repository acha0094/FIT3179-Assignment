Covid-19 Malaysia Data Analysis

(as of 19 September 2022)

Malaysia Total COVID-19 Cases Per Year

Malaysia was hit the hardest in the year 2021 due to the Covid-19 virus with an alarmingly high number of positive cases. An important thing to note is how the state of Perlis maintained a very low number of positive cases whilst the other states suffered significantly with massive amount of outbreaks.

Malaysia COVID-19 Daily New Cases

Malaysia encountered its largest wave of infection between February 2022 to May 2022 which fortunately, did not result in significant damages as a majority of the people have already been administered with vaccination shots.

Malaysia COVID-19 Daily Vaccination Doses Administered

We can observe that the first and second vaccine doses were administered during the period where the second largest wave of infection took place which suggests that, if the doses were administered at least two months earlier, the massive wave of infections could have been prevented.

Malaysia COVID-19 Cumulative Vaccination Shots

Based on the area chart above, we can see that around 72 million vaccination doses have been administered to the citizens of Malaysia. Making a conservative estimate of RM38.50 per dose, the government has already spent more than RM2.77 billion to purchase the Covid-19 vaccination shots.
